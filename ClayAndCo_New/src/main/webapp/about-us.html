OUR STORY

About Clay&Co.

Based in the UAE, our pottery studio is a space where creativity, craft, and community come together. Each piece we create is hand-crafted with care, celebrating the beauty of handmade ceramics and the joy of slow, thoughtful making. Alongside our collection of pottery available for purchase, we also offer hands-on classes where beginners and enthusiasts can learn the art of working with clay. Whether you're taking home a unique piece or shaping one yourself, everything we do is made with love and a passion for the craft.

Materials & Process

We work with durable stoneware clay and carefully selected, food-safe glazes chosen for their natural tones and everyday strength.

Each piece is shaped by hand—either on the wheel or through traditional hand-building techniques. After an initial bisque firing, our glazes are applied by hand before the pottery returns to the kiln for its final firing.

The result is pottery that's strong, functional, and full of character—made to be used, enjoyed, and part of daily life.
